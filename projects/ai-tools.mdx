A personal AI agent with automatic memory capture, knowledge compilation, and multi-platform integrations.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Memory System" icon="database">

    Nothing important falls through the cracks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Integrations" icon="plug">
