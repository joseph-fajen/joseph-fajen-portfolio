## Documentation + architecture + tooling

I write developer documentation for complex technical domains: blockchain, AI systems, REST APIs, video broadcast hardware. Twenty years in, across audiences from domain experts to high school students writing their first lines of code.

The work I'm proudest of is what happens when documentation alone isn't enough. The Marlowe smart contract platform needed 17,900 lines of developer docs and a 5-persona information architecture. Essential Cardano needed an AI assistant indexing 2,500 documents across eight sites. For Blockfrost, I built a Python toolkit to mine Discord conversations for documentation gaps.

I learn the domain. I work in the engineers' tools. And when the right tool doesn't exist, I make one.
