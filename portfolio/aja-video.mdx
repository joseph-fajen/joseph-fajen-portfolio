### How I worked

- Configured multi-product setups daily (encoders, routers, converters, monitors) — hands-on validation, not speculation
- Tracked signal flows across 4+ devices to verify documented behavior
- Served as informal QA, catching errors before release
- Reduced support ticket volume through comprehensive self-service documentation
