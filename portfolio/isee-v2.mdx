## The Project

ISEE v2 (Idea Synthesis and Extraction Engine) is a system I built in 2026 to solve a specific problem: how do you generate many ideas and reliably extract the best ones?

The approach: run prompts through multiple language models using multiple cognitive frameworks, then use structured debate to surface the strongest concepts.

---

## What I Delivered

### 1. Multi-Model Orchestration

Runs any prompt through 6 different language models, each applying 11 distinct cognitive frameworks — 66 combinations per prompt. This generates a wide range of perspectives on any problem.

### 2. Tournament Debate System

Synthesized ideas compete in a structured tournament debate to surface the strongest concepts. Adversarial evaluation is built in — ideas have to defend themselves against challenges.

### 3. Operations Dashboard

Real-time cost tracking across all model providers. Full observability into which models and frameworks contribute most to final outputs. Helps optimize for quality-per-dollar.
