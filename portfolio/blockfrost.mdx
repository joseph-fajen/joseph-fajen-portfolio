## The Project

[Blockfrost](https://blockfrost.io/) is a blockchain API provider for Cardano. During my time at Input Output Global, I was assigned for two to three months to assess and improve their documentation. The team had never had a technical writer; documentation had been a side project of the developer team.

I inherited a blank FAQ page.

---

## The Constraint

The signal was in Discord.

Early adopters and platform developers were having rich, technically dense conversations in the project's Discord channels. Real questions. Real confusion points. Real answers. None of it captured anywhere a future developer could easily find it.

The conventional sources weren't available. No support tickets to mine, no completed user research, no internal documentation to repurpose. The engineers were too busy shipping to sit for long interviews.

So the raw material existed — it was just in a format no documentation workflow knew how to ingest. And with months of backlog across multiple channels, reading it all by hand wasn't viable in the time I had.

---

## The Unconventional Move

I built a tool.

[ChatDoc-InsightMiner-PromptLab](https://github.com/joseph-fajen/ChatDoc-InsightMiner-PromptLab) is a Python toolkit that ingests chat logs into a vector database, runs specialized analytical prompts across three LLMs simultaneously (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini), and cross-validates the outputs to surface patterns rather than single-model artifacts.

For Blockfrost, that meant feeding it months of Discord history and asking it to identify the recurring questions, confusion points, and documentation gaps that real users were actually experiencing.

**Result:** [platform.blockfrost.io/faq](https://platform.blockfrost.io/faq)

## The Broader Pattern

Most documentation work starts after someone has decided what to document. Here, I had to figure that out first.

The toolkit is open source and applies to any documentation project with community conversation data — Discord, Slack, support channels, forum threads. The diagnostic approach generalizes beyond Blockfrost.

---

## Also Delivered: IA Improvements

Alongside the FAQ work, I assessed the broader platform documentation and restructured it to better serve developers at different stages of adoption — adding conceptual overviews, clarifying developer-facing docs, and improving navigation.
